Who is Eric Andre?

Eric Andre is an American actor, comedian, and television host. He is the creator, host, and co-writer of ‘The Eric Andre Show’ on Adult Swim.

Additionally, he played the role of Mike on the FXX series ‘Man Seeking Woman’.

Eric Andre: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Andre was born in Boca Raton, Florida on April 4, 1983. His father was a psychiatrist. He identifies himself as both black and Jewish.

Most of the information relating to his childhood and early life is not available at present. He holds the American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to a mixed ethnic background of Ashkenazi Jewish and Afro-Haitian.

In which school did Eric Andre attend?

Talking about his education, Andre graduated from Dreyfoos School of the Arts in West Palm Beach, Florida in 2001.

Later, Eric Andre studied at Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts, where he received a bachelor’s degree in double bass performance from Berklee College of Music in 2005.

Eric Andre: Profession, Career

Andre created the ‘ The Eric Andre Show’ in 2012 and is still airing. Furthermore, he is also the host of the show.

The show is a parody public access talk show on Cartoon Network’s late-night programming block Adult Swim and features pranks, shock humor, sketches, and celebrity interviews.

Andre has been part of several other movies and television series including ‘Research Unit’, ‘The Invention of Lying’, ‘The Big Bang Theory’, ‘Convention Panels’, ‘Should’ve Been Romeo’, ‘Don’t Trust the B—- in Apartment 23’, ‘The ArScheerio Paul Show’, ‘2 Broke Girls’, ‘Lucas Bros Moving Co’ and ‘Rough Night’ among others.

Andre received the Streamy Awards nomination in 2014 for ‘The ArScheerio Paul Show’ in the category of Best Ensemble Cast.
