How To Climb The Battlements In Hogwarts Legacy

July 2024 · 2 minute read

During the High Keep main quest in Hogwarts Legacy, players arrive at the locked Gatehouse, where they must climb the battlements to get inside and progress the quest.

However, climbing the battlements in Hogwarts Legacy is challenging as it requires a bit of platforming using multiple spells. Seeing that, we prepared this handy guide that explains how to climb the battlements in Hogwarts Legacy.

How To Climb The Battlements In Hogwarts Legacy

In Hogwarts Legacy, you can climb the battlements using a wooden box found behind a locked door on the left side. 

To get started, teleport to the Falbarton Castle Floo Flame teleport waypoint and make your way toward the right. There you will find climbable walls.

Use the directional and jump keys to climb up and walk towards the left side. There you will find a mechanism and a locked door with a wooden box.

Use the Depulso spell on the mechanism to open the locked door, and then use Wingardium Leviosa to pull the wooden box out of the room. After that, keep the Wingardium Leviosa active and release it when you place the box below the broken wall.

Now, use the Levioso spell to levitate the wooden box, use the forward + jump keys to climb the box, and then the ledge, completing the objective “Climb up the battlements.”

The next objective will ask you, “Enter the Gatehouse.” But the path is blocked by a wall made of wooden planks and a wooden box. Firstly, destroy the Wooden Planks using Depulso and then run towards the opposite side of the entrance.

You will find a window from where you can target the wooden box and use the Accio spell to pull it forward. Doing so will remove the box from the path.

Now, make your way inside and use Depulso on the mechanism and Accio on the gate lever to open the gates for Natsai Onai.

That concludes our guide on how to climb up the battlements in Hogwarts Legacy. For more on Hogwarts Legacy, keep reading The Games Cabin.
