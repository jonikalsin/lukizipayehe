What Soccer Team Does Matthew McConaughey Own?

August 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Matthew McConaughey, the renowned Hollywood actor, is one of the four new minority owners of the Major League Soccer (MLS) expansion franchise, Austin FC. 

Matthew McConaughey, a Texas native, has been unveiled as one of the four new minority owners of Austin FC, which is set to become MLS's 27th team in 2021.

The Ownership Group

The team of local investors, led by technology entrepreneur Eddie Margain, includes other notable figures such as Marius Haas, chief commercial officer at Dell Technologies, and Bryan Sheffield, an energy executive.

They join Austin FC's majority owner and chief executive, Anthony Precourt, to represent the club as governor on the MLS board.

McConaughey's Role as Minister of Culture

In an unprecedented move within the MLS, McConaughey has been given the distinctive title of "Minister of Culture" at Austin FC

. This role is designed to ensure that the club is deeply connected to the city of Austin and its vibrant culture.

As the Minister of Culture, McConaughey aims to align the team's identity with the spirit of Austin, reflecting its diversity, creativity, and vibrancy in the fan experience at the Q2 Stadium.

Vision for Austin FC

McConaughey's vision for Austin FC revolves around capturing the essence of Austin's culture and infusing it into the club's identity and fan experience.

He envisions the team and the city of Austin as mirror images of each other, with the diverse and vibrant spirit of the city resonating within the club.

His goal is to ensure that the fanbase reflects the diversity, creative colors, and vibrancy of Austin, Texas, creating a unique and inclusive environment at the Q2 Stadium.

McConaughey's Commitment to Austin FC

McConaughey's commitment to Austin FC goes beyond his role as a minority owner.

He has expressed his unwavering dedication to the club, eagerly embracing the challenge of building the team from the ground up.

Even before the team had a schedule, stadium, or other essential elements in place, McConaughey wholeheartedly embraced the opportunity to be part of the ownership group.

The Road to Debut

As Austin FC gears up for its debut in the MLS, McConaughey's enthusiasm is palpable.

The club is set to play its inaugural MLS match against LAFC, marking the realization of the vision that has been nurtured by McConaughey and the ownership group.

Their journey from vision to reality embodies the culmination of dedicated efforts and unwavering passion for soccer and the city of Austin.

Matthew McConaughey's ownership of Austin FC and his unique role as the Minister of Culture exemplify his deep-seated commitment to the club and the city of Austin.

His vision for aligning the team with the culture and spirit of Austin reflects a profound understanding of the community and a dedication to fostering a vibrant and inclusive soccer experience at the Q2 Stadium.

As Austin FC prepares to make its mark in the MLS, McConaughey's influence and passion are poised to leave an indelible imprint on the club's journey and the soccer landscape in Austin, Texas.
