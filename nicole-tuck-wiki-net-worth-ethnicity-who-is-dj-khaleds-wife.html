Who is DJ Khaled?

DJ Khaled was born in New Orleans, Louisiana USA, on 6 November 1975, so under the zodiac sign of Scorpio and holding American nationality – he is a DJ, record executive, songwriter, record producer, and a social media star.

DJ Khaled started off as a radio host in the 1990s, working for the “99 Jamz” Miami hip hop station – after the “Terror Squad” rap group heard his performances, they hired him as their DJ. This helped him gain recognition, and he eventually released his first album entitled “Listennn… the Album” in 2006 which soon became gold certified.

He went on to release albums “We the Best” in 2007, “We Global” in 2008, and “Victory” in 2010 – all of these albums ranked in the top 10 of the US Billboard 200, while his single “All I Do Is Win” was certified platinum three times. His “We the Best Forever” album in 2011 helped him to become popular worldwide – he released his next two albums “Kiss the Ring” and “Suffering from Success” in 2012 and 2013, and “I Changed a Lot” in 2015.

He rose to fame on the internet in January 2016, which led to the released of his ninth album “Major Key” in the same year – this album won him a Grammy nomination for the best rap album. His tenth album “Grateful” was released in 2017, and his “Father of Asahd” album followed in 2019 reaching #2 on the Billboard 200.

While DJ Khaled is mostly focused on making music, he is somewhat of a writer as well as he has written “The Keys” which has since become a bestseller – while he is not much of an actor, he has nevertheless managed to appear in “Spies in Disguise” movie shot in 2019 and will appear in “Bad Boys for Life” in 2020.
