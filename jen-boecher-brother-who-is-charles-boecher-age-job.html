Who Is Charles Boecher? Meet Jen Boecher Brother! His Age, Job

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Six new couples joined the cast of 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way Season 4, and it looks like one of the couples, Jen and Rishi, has a promising future ahead of them. However, they have one problem: Rishi’s parents in India have no idea about his relationship with Jen. Several of Jen and Rishi’s family members make their appearances in 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way Season 4 and Jennifer’s brother, Charles Boecher, is one of them.

Read the article below to learn more about 90 Day Fiance star Jen Boecher’s brother.

Meet Charles Boecher, 90 Day Fiance Star Jen Boecher Brother

On February 3, 2023, Jen uploaded a clip on her Instagram @jen_90day, where she could be seen visiting her brother’s farm Zion Oaks in Stillwell, Oklahoma. That IG post confirmed Charles’ appearance on 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way Season 4.

Three days later, Jennifer again shared a meme from the 90 Day Fiance News & Memes FB group made on Charles. In the caption, she wrote, “So proud of my brother with everything he and Tarra have done with their American Wagyu business & his winning the county commissioner election. He even has his own meme now! Also? Randomly, at least 420 people like it.”

90 Day Fiance star Jen works as a content creator and enjoys traveling. Jen, who was 46 at the time of filming, describes herself as a nomad and hates settling down from a young age. Since birth, she has lived in about 5 or 6 major cities.

Jen met Rishi while she was on her solo trip to India. She was passing by her hotel’s lobby when she noticed Rishi, who was there for a modeling gig. In their first conversation, Jen did not consider Rishi the type of person she would fall for. However, Rishi convinced her to hang out with him later that week, where she got to know the real him. It was that moment when Jen felt Rishi was meant for her.

One month after they started dating, Rishi proposed and Jen was over the moon to accept. Although Jen had to return to the US due to her visa restrictions, the reality TV star returned to India to marry her beloved.

Jen has had her fair share of heartbreaks in the past. Despite that, she felt confident about opening herself to Rishi. She was even ready to settle in India to live together with Rishi.

Although it looked like Jen and Rishi will be together for the rest of their life, their relationship had its first major hiccup when Jen learned that Rishi’s parents did not know about their relationship. On top of that, she also found out that Rishi’s parents were looking for a bride and would likely want him to have an arranged marriage with someone in their caste. Naturally, this created a rift between the two.

At the time of this writing, we did not know if the two were still together.

Charles Boecher Age

Born in 1979, Charles Boecher’s age was 43 as of February 2023.

Charles Boecher Job

Coming to Charles Boecher’s job details, his Facebook profile showed that he was a former senior intelligence analyst at US Army. While he was working there, he oversaw the management of the Reconnaissance, intelligence, and surveillance section. The guy also performed his standard intelligence and soldiering duties as an anti-terrorism officer Instructor, top-secret (SCI) global information systems operator, and trainer-instructor.

Prior to working in US Army, Charles was a noncommissioned infantry officer at the 3rd US Infantry Regiment (The Old Guard), where he served as a Ceremonial Escort to the President of The United States of America, taught and led soldiers in basic soldiering and maintaining modern infantry skills, and was a ceremonial functionary for the Sec. of Defense, the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs, and visiting State level diplomats.

Currently, Charles and his wife Tarra run Zion Oaks, a farm nestled in the community of Zion, in Stilwell Oklahoma. They raise American Wagyu cattle and are very passionate about the care that goes into managing their cattle and the experience of the consumers consuming their beef.

For education, Charles completed his Bachelor’s in Business Administration/Management at Marymount California University. He also studied at Cochise College and Delgado Community College.

Is Charles Boecher On Instagram?

Yes, you can find Charles Boecher on Instagram @charles_boecher, where he has 52 followers. More than Instagram, Charles is active on his Facebook handle.

Charles Boecher Wife

Charles Boecher is married to his wife, Tarra Boecher. As per his Facebook, the two tied the knot on March 10, 2018. On March 10, 2022, Tarra even took to Instagram to share pictures of her and Charles to mark their 4th wedding anniversary.

On June 23, 2021, Tarra wrote a sweet, heartfelt message for her husband, Charles. She shared a picture of her and Charles kissing each other on their lips and captioned it: ‘I use to believe the type of love this man encompasses only existed in dreams. I remember praying to God about everything I wanted in a soulmate… And then, this guy appeared nearly 4 years ago, when I least expected it. To say we fit like a glove is an understatement.”

Charles has three kids, Mikaela, Brock, and Charles Douglas Boecher, most likely from his previous marriage.
