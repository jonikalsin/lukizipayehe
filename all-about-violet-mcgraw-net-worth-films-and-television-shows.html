All About Violet McGraw Net Worth, Films, And Television Shows

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Violet McGraw is a well-known child actor for her roles in “Love,” “The Haunting of Hill House,” and her most recent film, “M3GAN.” She is slated to repeat her role in M3GAN’s sequel, M3GAN 2.

How old is Violet McGraw, actress for M3GAN?

Violet McGraw was born in California, United States, on April 22, 2011. Her age as of 2023 is 12 years old. She is the daughter of Colin and Jackie McGraw.

She is the youngest kid of four. Her siblings are all actors: Aidan McGraw of American Sniper, Jack McGraw of The Good Dinosaur, and Madeleine McGraw, who portrays Gwen in The Black Phone.

Violet McGraw’s Rise to Fame: Films and Television Shows

Violet McGraw had an early interest in acting and in the television series Love (2016).

She went on to star in a variety of additional television programs and made her cinematic debut in the 2018 film Ready Player One.

In addition to her work in television and film, she has been in countless commercials for a variety of businesses and products.

She was granted the role of Young Eleanor “Nell” in the 2018 season of the Netflix series The Haunting of Hill House. Her performance in that series established her as a renowned child actress.

She was selected to portray Violet in the 2019 film Doctor Sleep. McGraw makes her MCU debut as a teenage Yelena Belova in 2021’s Black Widow.

Jenny Vahn was her character in the 2021 film Separation. Then, she co-starred as one of the main characters in the January 2023 release of the Blumhouse/Universal Pictures technology horror film M3GAN. The film was a huge success, and her performance was praised by critics.

Violet McGraw’s Net Worth: How much did she earn from M3GAN?

As of 2023, Violet McGraw’s projected net worth is around $600,000. Over the years, she has earned a substantial sum of money for her efforts. Her work at M3GAN has also garnered her a substantial sum of money.

According to her contract, which was filed in court and obtained by TMZ, the actress was guaranteed four weeks of labor.

She was paid $3,932.50 per week for her services. She was granted an extra two weeks for the legally mandated quarantine in New Zealand, where the film was shot.

The contract stipulates that she is eligible for bonuses based on the film’s box office performance. Violet could be entitled to an additional $150,000, given that it has made more than $73 million in the United States.

McGraw is scheduled to repeat her part in the M3GAN sequel

Universal confirmed on January 18 that the sequel to the blockbuster film will be titled “M3GAN 2.0” and will be released on January 17, 2025.

In a recent interview with Variety, producers Jason Blum and James Wan confirmed that a sequel to the film will be produced. Blum said:

“After viewing the film for the first time, we had a strong feeling that a sequel would be successful. Therefore, we violated our cardinal rule and began discussing a sequel before the film’s premiere. I was so certain that we began considering a sequel earlier than usual.”
