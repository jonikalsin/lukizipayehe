Get to Know Esther Povitsky

June 2024 · 6 minute read

Esther Povitsky a quite popular name in the American TV industry, is a Californian comedian, podcast host, and actress. She is also known as "Little Esther" a stage name given by her fans.

The multi-talented Californian actress has a strong friendship with Benji Aflalo who shared his screen with her in Alone Together. There were some rumors that they were dating, but later on, they confirmed that they were best friends.

Early Life & Childhood

The Dollface actress was born on March 2, 1988, in  Stokie; a village in Illinois, USA, and raised by her father and mother. 

Her parents weren't that much exposed to the TV industry so they always motivated Esther to be a dancer. Though she liked dancing and music, she preferred to chase theatrical as her career.

Relationship With Dave King

Currently, Provitsky is engaged with Dave King, a comedy writer. When asked about her marriage she answered that for now, marriage is not what she has thought. 

Esther claims that her career is everything to her. In one of her joint podcasts Trash Tuesday w/ Annie, & Esther, & Khalyla she claimed that her fiance is obsessed with reading books.

Rumor About Dating Benji Aflalo

Due to their on-screen Romantic chemistry rumors of her relationship with Benji Aflalo were spread all over the internet. Fans were also too happy to see them as couples. But later on, the rumors are found to be wrong after they posted their video on youtube claiming they are just friends.

She also stated that she had dated a cool handsome boy in her high school but rejected to expose other information about him.

Is Benji Aflalo single?

Benji, being a standup comedian always posts sarcastic posts about his relationship on his social handles saying he married five times. 

To keep his personal life away from the limelight, he always makes a way to get rid of the questions about his relationship often with some great humorous jokes.

But recently, he opened himself in front of the public, posting his girlfriend's photo on his Instagram handle stating

Happy birthday to my beautiful girlfriend! Stop with the DMs, boys and girls! We’re taken ♥️

Net Worth - Why So Rich?

Esther Provitsky's estimated net worth is $5 million. The versatile figure has played in various super hit dramas like Alone Together(box office of around $7 million). 

She has multiple sources of income like podcasts, contracts for dramas and films, and branding in social media.

As mentioned earlier, she doesn't like to post about her personal life, so Information about her house, apartment, and real estate are a bit unknown. 

But she has posted a photo with her black luxury car of Pontiac Firebird on her social media. The car is worth around $33,000.

Own Brand: Sleepover By Esther

She owns a brand named Sleepover by Esther. The brand is about fashion and clothing. She promotes her brand on her socials and podcasts.

Family: Parents & Siblings

Esther has been uploading her parent's photos on her social media handles quite often. Her father's name is Morrie Provitsky and her mother is Marrie Provitsky. Her father is of Russian descent. 

In addition, she also has one sister whose identity has not been revealed to the public.

Education

The female star of Alone Together (2018) completed her high school graduation from 'Niles North High School' located in Skokie. After that, she went to join 'The University of Illinois' at Urbana–Champaign for her undergraduate degree. 

In the middle of her graduation, she decided to drop out of her college and started pursuing her career as an actress and standup comedian.

Pet Lover 

Little Esther is an animal lover. She had owned a  blonde white female cockapoo and named her Donut. But recently Duncan died due to some reasons which are not disclosed to the public. She had posted a post on her Instagram handle to relieve her pain from the huge loss, saying:

Goodbye to Duncan 💚
I’m sorry to hit you all with this. Open to any advice on dealing with this kind of loss. Thank you to this community of fans and friends for being a place for me to share. 💚

Role Model/ Inspiration

She considers Anna Nicole Smith as her role model. She even posted a post wishing Anna Nicole on Mother's Day.

some of you know her as anna nicole but to me, she’s a mom. happy mother’s day and thank you for making me the woman that i am today! 💞

Alone Together

 Povitsky is quite popular for her role in the 30-minute scripted comedy Alone Together. She scripted, co-created, produced, and starred in this sitcom with her friend Benji Aflalo.

Esther's and Benji's on-screen as well as real-life chemistry is one of the best things for their fans to watch.

Great Sense of Humour

Being a standup and a television comedian Esther is also hilarious in real life. She has got a great sense of humor. She posts some really humorous jokes based on her real life.

Her Collabs With Other Celebs

From 2012-2017 she had done Weird Adults with Little Esther she welcomed various famous celebrities like Joe Marcell, Duncan Trussell, Shasheer Zamata, Nicole Byer, and many more

She had also joined a podcast Good For You with Whitney Cummings. In one of her podcasts Glowing Up with Caroline Goldfarb, she said that she is a huge Taylor Swift fan and when she met with her she was so excited and the feeling was on another level.

Career: Her Rise In The Industry

The first role she got as an actress was as a teen model in the drama The Express: The Earnie Davis Story but the role was totally uncredited. She began her career as a podcast host in 2011 with the podcast Brode & Esther.

Before 2016 she was not that famous among people, but her role as "Maya" in the musical comedy-drama Crazy Ex-Girlfriend (2016-2019) was a turning point in her career. After that, in 2018 she played the role of protagonist in the hit sitcom Alone Together.

Her role as "Izzy" in Dollface was also admired by her fans and other people as well. In 2020 she played her debut comedy special Hot For My Name
