Leticia Gordon Obituary, Death Cause – In the quiet town of Sulligent, Alabama, a profound sense of loss envelops the community as they mourn the passing of a beloved member, Leticia Ann Gordon. A woman of grace, compassion, and warmth, Leticia peacefully departed this world on Tuesday, November 28, 2023, at the age of 62. In the coming days, we will gather to celebrate her life through memorial services on Monday, December 4, 2023, at 6:00 pm, at the Otts Funeral Home Chapel. The memorial service will be officiated by Bro. B.J. Mills, a cherished friend and spiritual guide.

Leticia’s journey was marked by a dedication to family, faith, and community. As we come together to remember and honor her, we will share stories, reflect on the memories we hold dear, and find comfort in the collective embrace of those who loved her. The memorial service serves not only as a farewell but as an opportunity to celebrate the vibrant life Leticia lived. Bro. B.J. Mills will guide us through moments of reflection, prayer, and the shared experience of saying goodbye to a woman whose impact reached far beyond her immediate circle.

In lieu of flowers, the family kindly requests that donations be made to the Union Chapel Baptist Church Building Fund, P.O. Box 23, Hamilton, AL, 35570. This gesture reflects Leticia’s unwavering commitment to her faith community and her desire to support the spiritual growth and well-being of others. Contributions to the building fund will be a lasting tribute to Leticia’s legacy, ensuring that her spirit lives on in the foundations of a place that held deep significance in her heart.

Condolences and special memories can be shared with the family at www.ottsfuneralhome.net. This digital platform offers a space for friends, family, and well-wishers to come together virtually, sharing their love and support during this time of remembrance. In a world that often feels vast, this online community becomes a testament to the enduring connections that Leticia fostered throughout her life.

As we navigate the emotions of grief and gratitude, let us remember Leticia for the love she gave, the joy she shared, and the impact she had on the lives she touched. In her absence, we find solace in the collective strength of community, drawing comfort from the memories and stories that bind us together. Leticia’s life was a testament to the power of kindness, the strength of faith, and the beauty of a life well-lived. Her departure leaves a void, but it also leaves us with the challenge and privilege of carrying forward the lessons she imparted through her words, deeds, and the love she shared.

The Otts Funeral Home Chapel, chosen as the venue for the memorial service, provides a serene and respectful space for the community to come together. It is a place where memories will be honored, tears shed, and hearts comforted. The chapel is more than a physical space; it becomes a vessel for the collective expression of love, gratitude, and farewell.
