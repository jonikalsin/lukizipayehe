Early Life

Varun Dhawan came to this world on April 24, 1987. He is originally from Bombay, Maharashtra, India. At present, he is 35 years old and his zodiac sign is Taurus. His parents are David Dhawan (father) a film director, and Karuna Dhawan (mother).

Career

Varun served as an assistant director to Karan Johar on the film ‘My Name Is Khan (2010).’ He made his acting debut in 2012 with Johar’s romantic comedy-drama ‘Student of the Year.’ For his performance, Varun earned him a Best Male Debut nomination at Filmfare.

After a year of his absence, he appeared in the romantic comedy film ‘Main Tera Hero (2014).’ And, the film emerged as a commercial success. Then, he starred in the romantic comedy ‘Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania.’

Varun played the role of Raghav “Raghu” Purohit in the crime thriller ‘Badlapur (2015)’. For his performance, Varun received a Filmfare Award for Best Actor nomination.

Also, Varun is famous for his roles in Dilwale (2015), the dance film ABCD 2 (2015), Dishoom (2016), Judwaa 2 (2017), and Sui Dhaaga (2018). Moreover, Varun received praise for playing an avenger in Badlapur (2015) and in the drama October (2018).

Dhawan is known for the period drama Kalank (2019) and Street Dancer 3D (2020), and comedy-drama Coolie No. 1 (2020). However, they were all critical and commercial failures, impeding his career.
