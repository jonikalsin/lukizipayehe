How to Unlock Silhouette Player Icons in Overwatch 2?

July 2024 · 2 minute read

After the launch of Overwatch 2 in October of 2022 there was an interested addition that fans were excited to have but had no ideas on how to obtain, the character-specific silhouette gamer icons. With the release of season three on the game nonetheless Blizzard has eventually obtained these icons and also announced how gamers will be eligible to obtain them for themselves. Just as other character icons in the game, the Overwatch silhouette gamer icons are received by earning games as those characters, precisely 15 wins as each. That’s the main path that Overwatch 2 tells you that you can obtain the icons, but there might be some other ways. How to Unlock Silhouette Player Icons in Overwatch 2?

Well, all of the icons can be obtained by winning 15 games as your favorite character (a counter that begins presently and isn’t retroactive to early seasons of Overwatch 2), the custom profile category of Overwatch 2 has a 50 coin cost tag on various of the silhouette player icons. When we tried to buy some of the icons ourselves via the game it didn’t go well nonetheless, so it’s not clear if this was done in glitch or if, at some point, the icons will be there for buying via the game. Right now, defeating others and winning the 15 games as a character are the only means to receive the silhouette player icons.

The inclusion of gamers being eligible to obtain the silhouette player icons isn’t the only thing Blizzard included, season 3 of Overwatch 2 is already shaping up to be its nicest yet. Key to season 3 are the modifications made to the battle pass, clearly disclosing that Blizzard is giving attention to the gamer base and making modifications on the basis of that fact. The free edition of the battle pass will have 1500 Credits for all gamers, giving them an obtainable currency, with which they can buy skins in the shop or hero gallery. Gamers that buy the premium battle pass will also be eligible to obtain an extra 500 Credits.
