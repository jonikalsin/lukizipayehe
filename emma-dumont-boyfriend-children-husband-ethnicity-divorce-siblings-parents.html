Who is Emma Dumont?

Emma Dumont is an actress, model, and dancer best known for her work in the TV series Bunheads, Aquarius, The Gifted, and the film Oppenheimer. She was born in Seattle, Washington, United States of America.

Emma Dumont – Age, Parents, Siblings

Emma Dumont was born on November 15, 1994, in Seattle, Washington, USA. Her full name is Emma Noelle Roberts. The names of her mother, father, and siblings are not known to the public. Also, there are no mentions of her parents’ profession.

Education, Ethnicity

For education, Emma Dumont attended Washington Middle School and later James A. Garfield High School. Due to her career in modeling and acting, she was homeschooled. She eventually attended Orange County High School of the Arts with a major in Music and Theater Conservatory.

While in school, in 2011, she was a member of the FIRST Tech Challenge team, and in 2012 a member of the FIRST Robotics Competition team. Dumont also participated in For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology.

Moreover, for her career as a ballet dancer, she studied at Pacific Northwest Ballet School, Cornish College of the Arts, Spectrum Dance Theatre School, and spent her summers at American Ballet Theatre, Joffrey Ballet School, and the Bolshoi Ballet Academy in Moscow, Russia.

Emma Dumont’s hobby also includes roller derby skating. She participated in the Los Angeles Derby Dolls training programs when she was 15 years old.

Despite her fame, her ethnicity has remained unknown.

Emma Dumont – Career

Early Days

Dumont was only 3 years old when she started training in ballet and was 6 years old when she began performing in community theater. She also worked as a child model.

As an actress, her debut role was in the 2009 film True Adolescents as Cara. After minor roles in the films Dear Lemon Lima (2009), and Nobody Walks (2012), she was cast as Melanie Segal in the TV series Bunheads. Emma Dumont portrayed the character for a total number of episodes 18 and it is considered her breakthrough work.

Films, TV Series

Over the years, Emma Dumont has starred in the films Nobody Walks (2012), Starving in Suburbia (2014), The Body Tree (2017), What Lies Ahead (2019), Wrong Turn (2021), Licorice Pizza (2021), Oppenheimer (2023), New Me, etc.

Moreover, she has portrayed characters in the episodes of the TV series Mind Games (2014), Aquarius (2015-2016), The Fosters (2016), T@gged (2017-2018), The Magicians (2017), Pretty Little Liars (2017), The Gifted (2017-2019), All Rise (2019), among others.

Co-stars

On the set of Oppenheimer, Emma Dumont has worked alongside Matthias Schweighöfer, Benny Safdie, Rob Morgan, Alex Wolff, and Olli Haaskivi.
