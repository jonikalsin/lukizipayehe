Wiki/Biography

Robert Sandberg was born on Sunday, January 17, 1993 (age: 26 years, as in 2019) in Sweden. He received his primary education from Falkenberg Hotel and Restaurant School, Falkenberg, Sweden. Since childhood, he wanted to be a chef. His zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Family

His father’s name is Hans Johan Sandberg and his mother’s name is Sandberg Monia. He has three sisters- Anna Sandberg, Fredrik Tagliatelle Sandberg, and Julle Sandberg. On March 14, 2019, he got engaged to Mia Khalifa. 

Career

He received an internship from Paris in culinary arts. Robert has also worked at Maaemo restaurant in Oslo, Norway. Thereafter, he moved to Los Angeles, USA. In 2016, he won the Worldchefs Hans Buckens- Young Chef Challenge.
